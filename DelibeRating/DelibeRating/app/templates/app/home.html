DelibeRating

A free web app to help you and/or your group decide where to eat.



Search
Tailor your searches how you like, and make a decision for yourself.

Randomizer:
Have a random place chosen for you, given a set of search criteria.

Stars:
These let the app know which places you really like, so that you never forget.

Thumbs up:
These let the app know what you like, so that it can suggest similar places.

Thumbs down:
These let the app know what you don't like, which disables most features for them.

Voting:
Create a group, and start a vote to decide where you want to eat with your group.
